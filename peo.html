Programme Educational Objectives

The programme will produce graduates:
PEO 1: To apply basic principles and practices of mathematical computing to solve technical problems.
PEO 2: To prepare graduates with solid foundation in Computer Engineering to analyze, design and implement software projects as well as to pursue higher studies.
PEO 3: To prepare students with latest skills in the field of software technologies supplemented with practical orientation to face challenges of modern computing industry.
PEO 4: To provide environment that fosters professional growth, communication skill, team work and ability to create awareness in society about applications of technology.
